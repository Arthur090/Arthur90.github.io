Edward Thomas Hardy

Born September 15, 1977

British actor, producer and scriptwriter. BAFTA Award winner and Oscar nominee.

From 1999 to 2004, Hardy was married to producer Sarah Ward. From 2005 to 2009 he was in a relationship with Rachel Speed, with whom he has a son, Louis (born 2008). On the set of the mini-series Wuthering Heights in 2009 he met actress Charlotte Riley. The couple married in July 2014. They have two children. Hardy had drug and alcohol addictions, but he got rid of them in 2003.
